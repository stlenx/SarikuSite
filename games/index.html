Breakout

Small atari breakout rip-off. With a system of power ups and buffs for a more exiting gameplay!

Green blocks duplicate your balls, red ones half them, pinks will make your platform half it's size and blue ones will make it double.

Insane mind blowing menu, nah but it's all made from scratch and drawn directly to the canvas which is pretty cool

Sandbox

Simple "cellular automata" sandbox game.

Select the element to use at the right, and your tool size with the slider at the top left. Deleting both floor and walls will make particles loop from the bottom back to the top of the screen, if you only remove the bottom layer they'll just fall.

You can hit "R" at any time to reset the play space.
